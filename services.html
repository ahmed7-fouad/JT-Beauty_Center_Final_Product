Sugaring ( sweet )

sugaring uses a sugar paste substance made up entirely of natural ingredients including sugar, water and lemon juice. This is different from wax which is pulled in the opposite direction of hair growth.It's better for sensitive skin as sugar doesn't stick to the skin like wax; also the sugar is not hot so it's best option done in hot weather.
